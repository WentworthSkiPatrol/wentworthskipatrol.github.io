Wentworth Update ~ 18 December 2024

Hello Wentworth Patrollers,

My weather app is showing 15 cm for Wentworth on Saturday, and things are looking really good for the hill to be open on opening day. What's more, the forecast continues to look good for the following days, with solid snowmaking lows for the next week. Whoohoo!

So now it's time for me to write my first plea of the season for everyone to dive into SUG and sign up for shifts. We need coverage over the holidays and we need to begin getting the rest of the season sorted out. New patrollers, keep an eye out for days when there are instructors available and sign up for training when you can. Returning folks, check your schedules and SUG and see what holes you can plug. And everyone, come on out to the hill and ski/ride/slide!

Cheers,
Alex

Rostering & Training

The standard expectation for Wentworth patrollers is 5 weekend/event shifts (around 35 - 40 hours), with no more than 3 of those being Saturdays. (Note that you could do 3 Saturday shifts plus 2 Saturday events if you wished.) But we are flexible—if this doesn't work for you, reach out to Alex (alex.rhinelander@skipatrol.ca) to arrange an alternate schedule.
SignUpGenius
  • SUG is now open for patrollers to sign up for their 5 shifts, with Patrol, Event and Training slots all posted.
  • Everything—rosters, training, competitions, socials—is on the same signup
  • There are 10 patrol slots each day, but we really want 6-7 patrollers on weekends and 2 on weekdays
  • Sign-ups are most useful when made well in advance.
  • There's an app for that!
