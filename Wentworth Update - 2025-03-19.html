End-of-Year Hike & Hangout

All patrollers (and spouses/partners/children/pets) are invited to meet Saturday at 1:30pm in the parking lot outside the rental shop for a hike up the snowshoe trail, followed by nachos at Ducky's at 4pm. You are welcome to join us at Ducky's even if you skip the hike.

This will be a great opportunity to return any borrowed uniforms/vests/bum bags, empty out your locker if you have one, and say farewell to your patrol friends until next ski season!

If you are planning to attend, sign up on SUG and indicate how many will be coming.

Thank you Jennifer for devising this plan!
Gear Return

All jackets, vests or any other items borrowed from the Zone MUST be returned for inventory and cleaning by April 15.

You have two choices for doing so (option #1 is preferred, but if you are unable to do so use #2):

  1. On your last shift of the year Saturday, March 22nd, return and sign in your gear. You must sign both the Return Form and the Loan Agreement (these are in a binder in the Patrol Hut) and then fold your jacket and put it in one of the red bags. 

  2. Arrange to return it directly by emailing or texting Alex at alex.rhinelander@skipatrol.ca / 902-456-5280. Location for dropoff is in Halifax.

Be sure to empty all pockets & remove name tags before returning gear.
