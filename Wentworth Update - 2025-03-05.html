Wentworth Update ~ 5 March 2025

Hello Wentworth Patrollers,

It's hard to believe that March is here, but it is, and the season's end is in sight. But there is still plenty of skiing left! If you have not yet filled your rosters, if you are looking for hours, or if you just want to get out there, now is the time! 

I have a number of announcements to share:

  • We will need race patrollers on Friday, Mar 21, as the Byrne Cup has been moved from this Friday to the 21st. Please come and help if you can. ~JamieD

  • On Sunday, March 23 we will hold an End of Year Barbecue at the Top Hut at noon. Please RSVP to jennifer.furlong@skipatrol.ca if you will be attending, and note any dietary requirements. ~Jennifer

  • Thanks to all who have contributed snacks and treats for the Top Hut! ~Jennifer

  • All borrowed uniforms, packs, etc. must be returned by the seaon's end. See below for more information. ~Rick

See you on snow,

Gear Return

All jackets, vests or any other items borrowed from the Zone MUST be returned for inventory and cleaning by April 15.

You have two choices for doing so (option #1 is preferred, but if you are unable to do so use #2):

  1. On your last shift of the year, return and sign in your gear. You must sign both the Return Form and the Loan Agreement (these are in a binder in the Patrol Hut) and then fold your jacket and put it in one of the red bags. 

  2. Arrange to return it directly by emailing or texting Alex at alex.rhinelander@skipatrol.ca / 902-456-5280. Location for dropoff is in Halifax.

Be sure to empty all pockets & remove name tags before returning gear.
