Wentworth Update ~ 26 February 2025

Hello Wentworth Patrollers,

Congrats and thanks to Mike Huggins and everyone who helped out with the Brunner—it was another very successful event. The event season is starting to wind down, but we do have a few good one left in the pipeline. This weekend is Freestyle and Snowboard Nova Scotia's Big Air and Slopestyle events, which are always a great scene. 

As I mentioned last week, PLEASE PLEASE visit SUG and sign up for shifts. We have done a great job of getting folks out on the hill, but not as great a job with signing up in advance. Events are still running so we need lots of coverage, March Break crowds are on their way, and a number of our high-hour patrollers are/will be travelling. All help in filling gaps is highly appreciated!

Lastly, there is one more previously-unpublished event happening Saturday—scroll to the bottom of the page for details. 

See you on snow,

Alex Rhinelander
Ski Wentworth Patrol Leader

World Telemark Day

Yes, that time has arrived again! Any and all who are willing to free their minds & heels are asked to meet at the top of the hill at noon on World Telemark Day (Saturday, March 1st), for a few runs and a photo op. 

Non-patrollers are more than welcome to join in—please help out by passing the word!
