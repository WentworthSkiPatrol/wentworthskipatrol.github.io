Lodge Day

Lodge Day is on Sunday, Dec. 8. Registration will run from 8:45 – 9:00 and Lodge Day itself will run from 9:00 – 3:05.

Coffee and lunch will be provided by the hill—thank you, Leslie!!!—but you should bring whatever snacks and nibbles you would like to consume during morning and afternoon breaks. 

If you are planning to patrol at Wentworth this year and will not be able to attend Lodge Day, please email Alex Rhinelander so we know your plans.

Passes

Wentworth is again using an online waiver and photo submission form for creating ski patrol passes. If you submit your information on this web form you will receive an email which lets you upload your preferred photo and sign the waiver. You will then be able to pick up your pass at the hill.
