Lodge Day

Lodge Day is on Sunday, Dec. 10. Registration will run from 8:45 – 9:00 and Lodge Day itself will run from 9:00 – 3:30. 

If you are planning to patrol at Wentworth this year and will not be able to attend Lodge Day, please contact Alex Rhinelander so we know your plans. 

We will be using one single integrated SUG page for all Wentworth signups this year. Lodge Day, Rostering, OnSnow Training, etc., ALL will be done on the same page. So, you can, if you wish, get a head start on rostering!

Passes

Wentworth is again using an online waiver and photo submission form for creating ski patrol passes. If you submit your information on this web form you will receive an email which lets you upload your preferred photo and sign the waiver. You will then be able to pick up your pass at the hill.
