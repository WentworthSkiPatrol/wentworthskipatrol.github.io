From the Hill ~ Terrain Report

The bordercross course is built on Pugwash and opened today.

Upper Hurricane currently has snowmaking setup, expect this in play for Saturday and hopefully the remainder of the season on a robust manmade base. It's been at least 4 years since we've opened Hurricane on manmade snow.

Yodeller (under Cobequid tower 5-8) has snowmaking in progress and should be in the mix soon. This will be the first time this has opened on manmade snow. I believe we're going to try this as an additional night skiing trail.

Headwall and Chute opened early this week.

Mogul Run is temporarily closed while the Canada Cup course is being built in Legacy Park.

It was briefly dumping in WW this afternoon and has been otherwise steadily snowing since around 1200, our forecast has snow continuing into tomorrow. We did some official work related exploratory natural snow terrain investigative skiing this afternoon and there's a good chance to get some or all of the Southside trails open tomorrow. Our existing natural snow base was thin but very firm, and the today snow is largely dry powish stuff which gets pushed around without much compaction VS the packy snowball making snow I was hoping for (blasphemous, yes) so we'll check it tomorrow to see if it is safe & reasonable to invite the public to ski and take it from there.

This week groomer conditions have been extremely good with loads of grip and very few "extra firm" spots.

We have a very busy event period coming up and I appreciate those who were already able to commit to an event spot on Signup Genius. Take a look at the remaining spots and consider working an event shift.

I've had a great time skiing this week and hope to see everyone out this weekend and am extra happy to see you out during the week!

~Josh

Rostering & Training

The standard expectation for Wentworth patrollers is 5 weekend/event shifts (around 35 - 40 hours), with no more than 3 of those being Saturdays. (Note that you could do 3 Saturday shifts plus 2 Saturday events if you wished.) But we are flexible—if this doesn't work for you, reach out to Alex (alex.rhinelander@skipatrol.ca) to arrange an alternate schedule.
